# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

## 简体中文（Chinese）

本应用高度重视您的隐私。

- 本应用不会访问互联网，也不会主动收集、上传、存储或共享您的任何个人信息、照片或使用数据。  
- 所有照片和视频的处理均在您的设备本地完成，过程中不会联网，也不会将任何数据发送给开发者或第三方。  
- 仅当您在系统设置中启用了 **“共享 iPhone/Mac/iPad 分析”**，并勾选了 **“与 App 开发者共享”** 时，Apple 才可能将包含匿名崩溃信息和基础使用数据的报告提供给开发者。这些报告仅用于改进 App 的稳定性和用户体验。

如您不希望共享任何分析数据，可在系统设置中关闭相关选项。

---

## English

**We respect your privacy.**

- This app does not access the internet and does not collect, upload, store, or share any personal information, photos, or usage data.  
- All photo and video processing is performed entirely on your device, without network access or external transmission.  
- Crash and usage reports may be shared **only if** you have enabled **“Share iPhone/Mac/iPad Analytics”** in your system settings and selected **“Share with App Developers.”** These reports, provided by Apple, are anonymous and used solely to improve app stability and performance.

You can disable analytics sharing at any time in your device’s system settings.
