# 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

## 简体中文（Chinese）

本应用高度重视您的隐私。

- 本应用不会主动访问互联网，也不会主动收集、上传、存储或共享您的任何个人信息、照片或使用数据。
- 若您在系统中启用了“存储空间优化”，当应用需要读取原始照片或视频时，操作系统可能会从 iCloud 下载这些文件。这一下载行为由系统负责，媒体文件始终在您的 Apple ID 环境中完成传输，转换过程仍在本地进行，不会发送给开发者或第三方。
- 在处理应用内购买或订阅时，应用会访问 App Store 相关 API 以验证或获取购买记录。该过程只与 Apple 的服务器通信，用于完成付款与恢复权限。
- 除上述由系统或 App Store 触发的网络请求外，应用不会进行任何联网操作。所有照片和视频的转换均在您的设备本地完成。
- 仅当您在系统设置中启用了 **“共享 iPhone/Mac/iPad 分析”**，并勾选了 **“与 App 开发者共享”** 时，Apple 才可能将包含匿名崩溃信息和基础使用数据的报告提供给开发者。这些报告仅用于改进 App 的稳定性和用户体验。

如您不希望共享任何分析数据，可在系统设置中关闭相关选项。

---

## English

**We respect your privacy.**

- The app does not proactively access the internet, nor does it collect, upload, store, or share your personal information, photos, or usage data.
- If “Optimize iPhone/Mac/iPad Storage” is enabled, the operating system may download originals from iCloud when the app needs to read photos or videos. This download is handled entirely by the system inside your Apple ID environment; conversion still happens locally and no media is sent to the developer or third parties.
- When processing in-app purchases or subscriptions, the app contacts the relevant App Store APIs to verify or retrieve purchase records. These requests communicate only with Apple servers to finalize payments or restore access.
- Aside from the system/iCloud downloads and App Store purchase validations described above, the app performs no network requests. All photo and video processing remains on your device.
- Crash or usage reports are shared **only if** you enable **“Share iPhone/Mac/iPad Analytics”** and select **“Share with App Developers.”** Apple provides anonymized reports solely to improve the app’s stability and performance.

You can disable analytics sharing at any time in your device settings.
